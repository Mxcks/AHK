# Black Armor Guide - OSRS

## Overview
Black armor is a popular early-game armor set that requires only 10 Defence to equip. It offers solid defensive bonuses and has a sleek, black appearance that many players prefer.

## Requirements
- **Defence Level:** 10
- **Total Cost:** ~7,000-9,000 coins for the full set

## Full Set Components
1. Black Full Helm
2. Black Platebody
3. Black Platelegs or Black Plateskirt (identical stats)
4. Black Kiteshield

---

## How to Obtain Each Piece

### 1. Black Full Helm
**Cost:** 1,372 coins

**Location:** Valaine's Shop (Champions' Guild)
- The Champions' Guild is located just south of Varrock
- Shop is upstairs in the guild

**Requirements:**
- 32 Quest Points to enter the Champions' Guild

**How to Get There:**
- **Varrock Teleport** (25 Magic) - Fastest method
- **Home Teleport to Lumbridge** - Run north to Varrock, then south to the guild
- **Walk** from any major city

**Alternative Method:**
- Kill monsters that drop black armor pieces (various monsters throughout Gielinor)

---

### 2. Black Platebody
**Cost:** 3,840 coins

**Location:** Horvik's Armour Shop (Varrock)
- Located just east of Varrock Square
- Easily accessible with no requirements

**How to Get There:**
- **Varrock Teleport** - Brings you right to Varrock Square
- **Canoe Travel** - Sail to Cook's Guild and run east
- **Home Teleport to Lumbridge** - Run north to Varrock

**Members Alternative:**
- Zenesha's Armour Shop in East Ardougne (same price: 3,840 coins)

---

### 3. Black Platelegs / Black Plateskirt
**Cost:** 1,920 coins

**Location:** Al Kharid (two shops next to each other)
- **Louie Legs' Platelegs Shop** - For platelegs
- **Ranael's Plateskirt Shop** - For plateskirt

**Note:** Platelegs and plateskirt have identical stats - choose based on preference

**How to Get There:**
- **Home Teleport to Lumbridge** - Run east across the bridge to Al Kharid
- **Amulet of Glory** (Members) - Direct teleport to Al Kharid
- **Walk** from Lumbridge (very close)

**Alternative:**
- Can also buy from Valaine in Champions' Guild (requires 32 Quest Points)

---

### 4. Black Kiteshield
**Cost:** 2,121 coins (if available in shop)

**This is the hardest piece to obtain!**

#### Free-to-Play Method:
**Kill Ice Giants** (Recommended for F2P and Ironman accounts)
- **Drop Rate:** 1/30
- **Locations:**
  - Asgarnian Ice Dungeon (south of Port Sarim)
  - White Wolf Mountain
- **Combat Level:** Ice Giants are level 53
- **Recommended Stats:** 40+ combat stats, bring food

#### Members Method:
**Saro's Quality Armour Shop** (Keldagrim)
- Located in southwest corner of Keldagrim
- **Problem:** Shop does NOT restock naturally
- Only available if another player has sold one to the shop
- Often out of stock

**How to Get to Keldagrim:**
- **Minecart System** from Grand Exchange
- **Fairy Ring** code DKS (near Rellekka Slayer Cave)
- **Slayer Ring Teleport** to Slayer Cave
- **Blast Furnace Minigame Teleport**

## Quick Shopping Route

### Fastest Route to Get Full Set:

1. **Start in Lumbridge**
   - Use Home Teleport if needed

2. **Go to Al Kharid** (5 minutes)
   - Run east across the bridge
   - Buy Black Platelegs/Plateskirt (1,920 coins)

3. **Teleport or Walk to Varrock** (5 minutes)
   - Buy Black Platebody from Horvik (3,840 coins)

4. **Go to Champions' Guild** (3 minutes)
   - South of Varrock
   - Buy Black Full Helm from Valaine (1,372 coins)
   - **Note:** Need 32 Quest Points first!

5. **Get Black Kiteshield**
   - **Option A:** Kill Ice Giants until you get the drop (~30 kills average)
   - **Option B:** Check Keldagrim shop (Members, often out of stock)
   - **Option C:** Buy from Grand Exchange (if you have the gold)

**Total Shopping Time:** ~15-20 minutes (excluding kiteshield farming)

## Why Get Black Armor?

### Pros:
- **Low Requirements:** Only 10 Defence needed
- **Affordable:** Total cost under 10,000 coins
- **Good Early-Game Defence:** Solid upgrade from Iron/Steel
- **Stylish Appearance:** Popular black color scheme
- **Easy to Obtain:** Most pieces available in shops

### Cons:
- **Cannot be Smithed:** Unlike other metal armors
- **Kiteshield is Tedious:** Requires grinding Ice Giants or luck in shops
- **Quickly Replaced:** Mithril (20 Def) and Adamant (30 Def) are better

### When to Use:
- Early to mid-game combat training
- Levels 10-30 Defence
- Budget-friendly option for new players
- Fashion/nostalgia purposes

---

## Alternatives to Consider

**Lower Level:**
- **Steel Armor** (5 Defence) - Cheaper, easier to obtain
- **Iron Armor** (1 Defence) - Very cheap starter armor

**Same Level:**
- **White Armor** (10 Defence) - Requires quests, has Prayer bonus
- **Batwing Armor** (10 Defence, 1 Magic) - For magic users

**Higher Level:**
- **Mithril Armor** (20 Defence) - Better stats, can be smithed
- **Adamant Armor** (30 Defence) - Much better stats
- **Rune Armor** (40 Defence) - Best F2P armor

---

## Tips and Tricks

1. **Save Money on Kiteshield:** Kill Ice Giants instead of buying from GE
2. **Quest First:** Get 32 Quest Points before shopping for the helm
3. **Bring Exact Change:** Have at least 10,000 coins ready
4. **Use Teleports:** Saves time when shopping at multiple locations
5. **Consider Skipping:** If you have 20+ Defence, go straight to Mithril
6. **Ironman Players:** Must kill Ice Giants for kiteshield (can't trade)
