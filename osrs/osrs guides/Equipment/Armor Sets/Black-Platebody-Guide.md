## Where to Buy

### Option 1: Horvik's Armour Shop, Varrock (BEST) ‚≠ê

**Location:** East of Varrock Square  
**Price:** 3,840 coins  
**Stock:** Always available  
**Requirements:** None

**How to Get There:**
1. **Varrock Teleport** (25 Magic, 1 Law, 3 Air, 1 Fire)
   - Teleports to Varrock Square
   - Run east to shop
   - **Time:** 30 seconds

2. **Home Teleport to Lumbridge**
   - Run north to Varrock
   - Run to east side
   - **Time:** 5 minutes

3. **Walk from Grand Exchange**
   - Run south through Varrock
   - Shop is on east side
   - **Time:** 2 minutes

**Shop Details:**
- Shop Name: Horvik's Armour Shop
- NPC: Horvik
- Always in stock
- No requirements to enter
- Can sell items here too

## Step-by-Step Purchase Guide

### Fastest Method (5 Minutes):

**Preparation:**
1. Have 4,000 coins ready
2. Have 10 Defence level

**Steps:**
1. Teleport to Varrock (or walk from Lumbridge)
2. Run east to Horvik's Armour Shop
3. Talk to Horvik
4. Select "What do you have for sale?"
5. Right-click Black Platebody ‚Üí Buy 1
6. Confirm purchase (3,840 coins)
7. Equip it!

**Total Time:** 5 minutes  
**Total Cost:** 3,840 coins

## When to Buy

### Best Time to Get Black Platebody:

**Immediately at 10 Defence:**
- Huge upgrade from Iron/Steel
- Affordable at low levels
- Will last until 20 Defence (Mithril)

**Skip if:**
- You have 20+ Defence (get Mithril instead)
- You're rushing to higher levels
- You have better armor already

## Tips and Tricks

### üí° Pro Tips:
