## Fast Shopping Route (15 Minutes)

### Stop 1: Al Kharid (Start Here)

**Item:** Black Platelegs  
**Shop:** Louie Legs' Platelegs Shop  
**Price:** 1,920 gp

**How to Get There:**
- Home Teleport to Lumbridge
- Run east across bridge
- **Time:** 2 minutes

**What to Do:**
1. Enter Louie Legs' shop (south-east Al Kharid)
2. Buy Black Platelegs (1,920 gp)
3. ‚úÖ Item 1 complete!

---

### Stop 2: Varrock (5 Minutes from Al Kharid)

**Item:** Black Platebody  
**Shop:** Horvik's Armour Shop  
**Price:** 3,840 gp

**How to Get There:**
- Varrock Teleport (if you have 25 Magic)
- OR walk north-west from Al Kharid
- **Time:** 5 minutes walk, instant teleport

**What to Do:**
1. Go to east side of Varrock Square
2. Enter Horvik's Armour Shop
3. Buy Black Platebody (3,840 gp)
4. ‚úÖ Item 2 complete!

---

### Stop 3: Champions' Guild (3 Minutes from Varrock)

**Item:** Black Full Helm  
**Shop:** Valaine's Shop  
**Price:** 1,372 gp  
**Requirement:** 32 Quest Points

**How to Get There:**
- Run south from Varrock
- Champions' Guild is just south of Varrock
- **Time:** 3 minutes

**What to Do:**
1. Enter Champions' Guild (need 32 QP!)
2. Go upstairs
3. Talk to Valaine
4. Buy Black Full Helm (1,372 gp)
5. ‚úÖ Item 3 complete!

---

### Stop 4: Ice Giants (30-60 Minutes)

**Item:** Black Kiteshield  
**Method:** Kill Ice Giants  
**Drop Rate:** 1/30

**Locations:**

**Option A: Asgarnian Ice Dungeon** (Recommended)
- South of Port Sarim
- Enter dungeon
- Ice Giants inside
- **Time:** 30-60 minutes for drop

**Option B: White Wolf Mountain**
- Between Taverley and Catherby
- Ice Giants on mountain
- More dangerous

**What to Do:**
1. Go to chosen location
2. Kill Ice Giants (level 53)
3. Wait for Black Kiteshield drop (1/30 chance)
4. ‚úÖ Item 4 complete!

**Combat Recommendations:**
- 40+ combat stats
- Bring food
- Use Protect from Melee prayer if you have it

## Alternative: Skip the Shield

### If You Don't Want to Farm Ice Giants:

**Option 1:** Buy from Grand Exchange
- Price varies (usually 2,000-3,000 gp)

**Option 2:** Use Different Shield
- Mithril Kiteshield (cheaper, similar stats)
- Skip shield entirely

**Option 3:** Come Back Later
- Get shield when you're higher level
- Use 3-piece set for now
