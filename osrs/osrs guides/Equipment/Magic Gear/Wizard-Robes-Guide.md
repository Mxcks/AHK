## Method 1: Get FREE Robes (Recommended)

### Location: Wizards' Tower

**Where:** South of Draynor Village, south of Lumbridge

**How to Get There:**
1. Start in Lumbridge
2. Run west to Draynor Village (5 min)
3. Run south from Draynor to Wizards' Tower (2 min)
4. **Total time:** 7 minutes from Lumbridge

### What to Do:

**Step 1: Enter the Tower**
1. Go to the Wizards' Tower
2. Enter through the front door

**Step 2: Go to Basement**
1. Find the ladder going down
2. Climb down to basement

**Step 3: Find the Magic Store**
1. Look for a room with items on tables
2. You'll see wizard robes lying around

**Step 4: Pick Up Robes**
1. Right-click wizard hat ‚Üí Take
2. Right-click wizard robe top ‚Üí Take
3. Right-click wizard robe bottom ‚Üí Take
4. **Done! You have full wizard outfit!**

### Respawn Times:
- If robes aren't there, someone else took them
- They respawn every few minutes
- Hop to another world (World Switcher)
- Or wait 2-3 minutes

**A:** Hop to another world or wait 2-3 minutes for respawn.

### Q: Are blue robes better than wizard robes?
**A:** Same stats! Just different color. Get whichever is cheaper.

### Q: Should I buy black robes?
**A:** Only if you want to look cool. Same stats as blue/wizard robes.

### Q: What's the best beginner magic armor?
**A:** FREE wizard robes from Wizards' Tower!
