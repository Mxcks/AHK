### How to Download the Map Image:

**To save a map image to this folder for AI reference:**

1. **Visit the Official OSRS World Map:**
   - Go to: https://www.runescape.com/oldschool/world-map
   - Click the map image
   - Right-click and select "Save image as..."
   - Save to this `Maps/` folder as `OSRS-World-Map.png`

2. **OR use the OSRS Wiki Map:**
   - Go to: https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/World_map
   - Click "Full resolution image" link
   - Right-click the image and "Save image as..."
   - Save to this `Maps/` folder as `OSRS-World-Map.png`

3. **OR use Reddit Community Maps:**
   - Go to: https://www.reddit.com/r/OSRSmaps/
   - Find high-resolution map posts
   - Download and save to this `Maps/` folder

**Once downloaded, the AI can reference the image in future conversations!**

## üìç Key Locations for Beginners

### Starting Area: Lumbridge

**Lumbridge** is where you start after Tutorial Island!

**Key Locations in Lumbridge:**
- **Lumbridge Castle** - Center of town, has bank upstairs
- **General Store** - Buy basic supplies
- **Cow Pen** - North-east, kill cows for money/training
- **Chicken Coop** - West of castle, kill chickens
- **Lumbridge Swamp** - South, fish shrimp here
- **Lumbridge Furnace** - South-west of castle

**Directions FROM Lumbridge:**
- **North** ‚Üí Varrock (5 min walk)
- **West** ‚Üí Draynor Village (3 min walk)
- **South** ‚Üí Lumbridge Swamp
- **East** ‚Üí Al Kharid (cross bridge, 2 min walk)

---

### Major Cities

#### 1. Varrock
**Location:** North of Lumbridge  
**Travel Time:** 5 minutes walk from Lumbridge  
**Teleport:** Varrock Teleport (25 Magic)

**Key Locations:**
- **Grand Exchange** - North-west, buy/sell items
- **Varrock Square** - Center of city
- **Varrock East Bank** - East side
- **Varrock West Bank** - West side
- **Zaff's Staffs** - West side, buy air staff here!
- **Aubury's Rune Shop** - East side, buy runes
- **Horvik's Armour Shop** - East side, buy Black Platebody
- **Thessalia's Clothes** - South-east, buy robes

**Directions FROM Varrock:**
- **South** ‚Üí Lumbridge (5 min)
- **West** ‚Üí Barbarian Village (3 min)
- **North** ‚Üí Wilderness
- **East** ‚Üí Lumber Yard

---

#### 2. Falador
**Location:** West of Lumbridge/Varrock  
**Travel Time:** 10 minutes walk from Lumbridge  
**Teleport:** Falador Teleport (37 Magic)

**Key Locations:**
- **Falador Park** - Center, has trees
- **Falador East Bank** - East side
- **Falador West Bank** - West side
- **Party Room** - North-west
- **Mining Guild** - South (60 Mining required)

---

#### 3. Draynor Village
**Location:** West of Lumbridge  
**Travel Time:** 3 minutes walk from Lumbridge  
**Teleport:** Draynor Manor Teleport (requires Arceuus spellbook)

**Key Locations:**
- **Draynor Bank** - North side (closest bank to Lumbridge!)
- **Draynor Market** - Center, various shops
- **Wizards' Tower** - South, get FREE wizard robes here!
- **Draynor Manor** - North, spooky house

---

#### 4. Al Kharid
**Location:** East of Lumbridge (across bridge)  
**Travel Time:** 2 minutes walk from Lumbridge  
**Teleport:** None (low level)

**Key Locations:**
- **Al Kharid Palace** - Center
- **Al Kharid Bank** - North side
- **Louie Legs' Shop** - South-east, buy Black Platelegs here!
- **Furnace** - North, for smithing
- **Tannery** - South-east, tan hides

**Note:** 10 gp toll to cross bridge (or complete Prince Ali Rescue quest for free passage)

---

### Important Beginner Locations

#### Barbarian Village
**Location:** North of Lumbridge, west of Varrock  
**Travel Time:** 5 minutes from Lumbridge  

**Key Locations:**
- **Stronghold of Security** - Hole in center of village
  - **IMPORTANT:** Get 11,250 coins here! (See First 10,000 Coins Guide)
- **Barbarian Outpost** - North (requires Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl)

---

#### Wizards' Tower
**Location:** South of Draynor Village  
**Travel Time:** 7 minutes from Lumbridge  

**Key Locations:**
- **Basement** - Get FREE wizard robes here!
- **2nd Floor** - Wizard Mizgog (Imp Catcher quest)
- **Rune Mysteries Quest** - Start here

---

#### Port Sarim
**Location:** South-west of Draynor Village  
**Travel Time:** 10 minutes from Lumbridge  

**Key Locations:**
- **Docks** - Ships to various islands
- **Magic Shop** - Buy runes
- **Food Shop** - Buy food
- **Asgarnian Ice Dungeon** - South, Ice Giants drop Black Kiteshield

---

#### Edgeville
**Location:** North of Barbarian Village  
**Travel Time:** 8 minutes from Lumbridge  
**Teleport:** Amulet of Glory (30 Magic to charge)

**Key Locations:**
- **Edgeville Bank** - Very popular bank
- **Edgeville Dungeon** - North of bank, Hill Giants inside
- **Wilderness** - North (dangerous PvP area!)

## üß≠ Navigation Guide

### How to Get Anywhere from Lumbridge:

**To Varrock:**
1. Exit Lumbridge Castle
2. Run north
3. Keep running north past goblins
4. You'll reach Varrock (5 min)

**To Draynor Village:**
1. Exit Lumbridge Castle
2. Run west
3. Cross the bridge
4. Keep running west (3 min)

**To Al Kharid:**
1. Exit Lumbridge Castle
2. Run east
3. Cross the bridge (10 gp toll or free with quest)
4. You're in Al Kharid (2 min)

**To Barbarian Village:**
1. Run north to Varrock
2. From Varrock, run west (3 min)
3. OR: Run north from Lumbridge, then west before Varrock

**To Wizards' Tower:**
1. Run west to Draynor Village
2. From Draynor, run south (2 min)
3. You'll see the tall tower

**To Edgeville:**
1. Run north to Varrock
2. From Varrock, run north-west
3. OR: Go to Barbarian Village, then north

## üí° Map Tips

### In-Game Map Tips:
1. **Press Ctrl+M** to open world map
2. **Search bar** at bottom - type location names
3. **Zoom** with mouse wheel
4. **Click and drag** to move around
5. **Yellow arrow** shows your current location

### Navigation Tips:
1. **Use Home Teleport** - It's free!
2. **Follow roads** - They connect cities
3. **Use minimap** - Shows nearby area
4. **Set destination** - Right-click minimap to set waypoint
5. **Ask other players** - Most are helpful!

### Getting Lost?
1. Open world map (Ctrl+M)
2. Look for yellow "You are here" arrow
3. Search for your destination
4. Follow the path on minimap
