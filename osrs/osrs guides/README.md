### Fastest Ways to Get Started

#### Black Armor (10 Defence Required)
- **Black Platebody**: Horvik's Shop in Varrock (3,840 gp)
- **Black Platelegs**: Al Kharid shops (1,920 gp)
- **Black Full Helm**: Champions' Guild (1,372 gp, requires 32 Quest Points)
- **Black Kiteshield**: Kill Ice Giants or buy from Keldagrim (hardest piece)

#### Magic Training Quick Start
1. Complete Witch's Potion + Imp Catcher (instant level 10)
2. Low Level Alchemy to 55
3. High Level Alchemy from 55+ (passive, profitable)
4. Bursting/Barraging at 62+ (fastest active method)

#### Top 5 Fastest Skills to 99
1. **Cooking** (40-60 hours) - Fast and profitable
2. **Firemaking** (50-70 hours) - Wintertodt is fun and profitable
3. **Fletching** (40-60 hours) - Very fast XP rates
4. **Combat Skills** (100-150 hours each) - AFK at Nightmare Zone
5. **Thieving** (60-80 hours) - Blackjacking or Ardougne Knights
