## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### Step 1: Start the Quest
1. Go to **Wizards' Tower** (south of Draynor Village)
2. Enter the tower
3. Climb stairs to **2nd floor [UK] / 3rd floor [US]**
4. Talk to **Wizard Mizgog**
5. Ask about his problem
6. He'll tell you imps stole his magic beads
7. Agree to collect 4 beads

## Method 2: Kill Imps (FREE - 30-90 Minutes)

### Drop Information:
- **Drop Rate:** 5/128 per bead (~3.9% chance)
- **Average Kills:** 100-150 imps for all 4 beads
- **Imp Level:** 2 (very weak)
- **Imp HP:** 8

### Best Imp Locations:

#### Location 1: East of Ardougne Monastery (Members) ‚≠ê
- **Pros:** Many imp spawns, safe area
- **Cons:** Members only
- **How to Get There:** Teleport to Ardougne, run east

#### Location 2: Karamja Volcano (Top)
- **Pros:** Multiple imp spawns, F2P accessible
- **Cons:** Level 14 scorpions also spawn here
- **How to Get There:** Karamja, climb volcano
- **‚ö†Ô∏è Warning:** Bring food if low combat!

#### Location 3: Southern Falador Entrance (F2P) ‚≠ê
- **Pros:** F2P, near bank, safe
- **Cons:** Fewer spawns than other locations
- **How to Get There:** Falador south gate

#### Location 4: Kastori Basement (Members)
- **Pros:** Underground, multiple spawns
- **Cons:** Members only, harder to access
- **How to Get There:** Kastori, go to basement

### Imp Hunting Tips:

**Combat Strategy:**
- Use **Magic** to train while collecting beads
- Use **Ranged** to attack from distance
- Melee works but you'll chase imps more

**Imp Behavior:**
- Every 30-120 seconds: 25% chance to teleport away
- Teleports in puff of smoke
- Teleports short distance
- Use ranged combat to minimize downtime

**Recommended Spells:**
- Wind Strike (level 1 Magic)
- Fire Strike (level 13 Magic)
- Any combat spell

**Inventory Setup:**
- Weapon and runes/ammo
- Food (if low level)
- Empty space for bead drops
