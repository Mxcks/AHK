## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### Step 1: Start the Quest
1. Go to **Hetty's house** in south-east Rimmington
2. Talk to **Hetty**
3. Choose: "I want to know more about the black arts"
4. She'll ask for 4 ingredients

---

### Step 2: Get Rat's Tail

**Location:** Archery shop west of Hetty's house

**Instructions:**
1. Exit Hetty's house
2. Run west to the archery shop
3. Kill a **level 1 rat** (NOT giant rats!)
4. Pick up the rat's tail

**‚ö†Ô∏è Important:**
- You MUST start the quest first!
- Only level 1 rats drop rat's tail
- Giant rats do NOT drop rat's tail
- 100% drop rate from level 1 rats

**Alternative Locations:**
- Around Rimmington houses
- Inside buildings in Rimmington

---

### Step 3: Get Burnt Meat

#### Method A: Kill and Burn (Recommended)

**Option 1: Kill a Cow**
1. Go to cow pen south of Falador
2. Kill a cow
3. Pick up raw beef
4. Use raw beef on Hetty's fireplace
5. If it cooks successfully, use cooked meat on fire again to burn it

**Option 2: Kill a Giant Rat**
1. Go north of chapel graveyard (south-east of Rimmington)
2. Kill a giant rat
3. Pick up raw rat meat
4. Cook and burn it on any fire

#### Method B: Buy Raw Meat
1. Go to Wydin's Food Store in Port Sarim
2. Buy raw meat for 3 coins
3. Burn it on a fire

**üí° Cooking Tips:**
- Level 1 Cooking = Very likely to burn automatically
- Higher Cooking = Use cooked meat on fire to intentionally burn
- Can use any fire or range
- Hetty's fireplace works perfectly

---

### Step 4: Get Eye of Newt

**Location:** Betty's Magic Shop in Port Sarim  
**Cost:** 3 coins

**Instructions:**
1. Go to Port Sarim (east of Rimmington)
2. Look for shop with **Fire Rune symbol** on minimap
3. Talk to Betty
4. Buy 1 Eye of newt for 3 coins

**Alternative:**
- Buy from Grand Exchange (3-5 coins)
- Stock up on extras for future magic training!

---

### Step 5: Get Onion

#### Option A: Rimmington Field (Closest)
1. Go directly north of Rimmington
2. Look for onion field east of Melzar's Maze
3. Pick up 1 onion from the ground

#### Option B: Lumbridge Farm
1. Go to Fred the Farmer's farmhouse
2. Go to backyard (west of sheep pen)
3. Pick up 1 onion

#### Option C: Grand Exchange
- Buy for 10-20 coins

---

### Step 6: Complete the Quest

1. Return to **Hetty's house** with all 4 items:
   - ‚úì Rat's tail

   - ‚úì Onion

2. Talk to Hetty
