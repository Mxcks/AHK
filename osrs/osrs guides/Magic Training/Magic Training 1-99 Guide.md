## Quest Guides for Early Magic XP

### Witch's Potion Quest Guide

**Quest Overview:**
- **Difficulty:** Novice
- **Length:** Very Short (5-10 minutes)
- **Requirements:** None
- **Magic XP Reward:** 325 XP
- **Quest Points:** 1

**Start Location:** Hetty's house in Rimmington (south of Falador, west of Port Sarim)

---

#### Items Required:
- [ ] Burnt meat
- [ ] Eye of newt
- [ ] Onion
- [ ] Rat's tail (obtained during quest)

**Note:** You can gather all items except the rat's tail BEFORE starting the quest!

---

#### Step-by-Step Walkthrough:

**Step 1: Getting to Rimmington**

**Fastest Methods:**
- **Teleport to House** (requires 40 Magic, 1 Law rune, 1 Air rune, 1 Earth rune)
  - Default house location is Rimmington
- **Skills Necklace** to Crafting Guild (Members)
  - Run south to Rimmington
- **Walk from Falador**
  - Exit south gate, follow road west to Rimmington

**Step 2: Start the Quest**

1. Go to Hetty's house in **south-east Rimmington**
2. Talk to Hetty
3. Tell her you want to know more about the **black arts**
4. She'll ask you to gather 4 ingredients for her potion

---

#### Gathering the Ingredients:

**1. Rat's Tail**

**Location:** Kill a level 1 rat
- **Best spot:** Archery shop west of Hetty's house
- **Alternative:** Any level 1 rat in Rimmington

**Important Notes:**
- You MUST start the quest first to get rat's tail drops!
- Only level 1 rats drop rat's tail (NOT giant rats!)
- 100% drop rate from level 1 rats

**How to Get There:**
- Exit Hetty's house
- Run west to the archery shop
- Kill rats inside or around the building

---

**2. Burnt Meat**

**Option A: Burn It Yourself (Recommended)**

1. Kill a cow or giant rat for raw meat
   - **Cows:** Cow pen south of Falador
   - **Giant Rats:** North of chapel graveyard (south-east of Rimmington)

2. Cook the raw meat
   - Use meat on Hetty's fireplace (in her house)
   - Use meat on any fire or range
   - If you accidentally cook it successfully, use cooked meat on fire again to burn it

**Option B: Buy Raw Meat**
- Buy from Wydin's Food Store in Port Sarim (3 coins)
- Then burn it on a fire

**Cooking Levels:**
- Level 1 Cooking: Very likely to burn
- Higher Cooking: May need to intentionally burn by using cooked meat on fire

---

**3. Eye of Newt**

**Location:** Magic shop in Port Sarim
**Cost:** 3 coins

**How to Get There from Rimmington:**
1. Run east from Rimmington to Port Sarim
2. Look for the shop with a **Fire Rune symbol** on the minimap
3. Talk to Betty at the magic shop
4. Buy 1 Eye of newt for 3 coins

**Alternative:**
- Buy from Grand Exchange (usually 3-5 coins)
- Stock up on extras for future use!

---

**4. Onion**

**Option A: Rimmington Field (Closest)**
- Field directly **north of Rimmington**
- East of Melzar's Maze
- Just pick one from the ground

**Option B: Lumbridge Farm**
- Backyard of Fred the Farmer's farmhouse
- West of the sheep pen
- Multiple onions spawn here

**Option C: Grand Exchange**
- Buy for ~10-20 coins

---

**Step 3: Complete the Quest**

1. Return to Hetty's house with all 4 items:
   - ✓ Rat's tail
   - ✓ Burnt meat
   - ✓ Eye of newt
   - ✓ Onion

2. Talk to Hetty
3. She'll add the ingredients to her cauldron
4. **Drink from the cauldron** when she tells you to
5. Quest complete!

---

#### Rewards:
- ✅ 1 Quest Point
- ✅ 325 Magic XP (takes you from level 1 to level 2)

---

#### Quick Route Summary:

**Efficient Route (10 minutes):**
1. Buy Eye of newt in Port Sarim (3 coins)
2. Pick onion north of Rimmington
3. Go to Hetty's house and start quest
4. Kill rat in archery shop for rat's tail
5. Kill cow/giant rat for raw meat
6. Burn meat on Hetty's fireplace
7. Talk to Hetty and give her all items
8. Drink from cauldron

**Total Cost:** ~3-10 coins

### Imp Catcher Quest Guide

**Quest Overview:**
- **Difficulty:** Novice
- **Length:** Short (15-60 minutes depending on luck)
- **Requirements:** None
- **Magic XP Reward:** 875 XP
- **Quest Points:** 1
- **Additional Reward:** Amulet of Accuracy

**Start Location:** Wizard Mizgog on the 2nd floor of the Wizards' Tower (south of Draynor Village)

---

#### Items Required:
- [ ] Black bead
- [ ] Yellow bead
- [ ] Red bead
- [ ] White bead

**Note:** You can collect all beads BEFORE starting the quest to save time!

---

#### Step-by-Step Walkthrough:

**Step 1: Getting to Wizards' Tower**

**Fastest Methods:**
- **Necklace of Passage** (Members) - Direct teleport to Wizards' Tower
- **Amulet of Glory** to Draynor Village
  - Run south across the bridge
- **Home Teleport to Lumbridge**
  - Run south, then west across bridge
- **Fairy Ring DIS** (Members)
  - Requires 49 Fairy Tale Part II progress

**Step 2: Start the Quest**

1. Enter the Wizards' Tower (south of Draynor Village)
2. Climb the stairs to the **2nd floor [UK] / 3rd floor [US]**
3. Talk to **Wizard Mizgog**
4. Ask him questions about his problem
5. He'll tell you that imps stole his magic beads
6. Agree to help him collect 4 beads

---

#### Obtaining the Beads:

**Option A: Buy from Grand Exchange (FASTEST)**

**Current Prices (approximate):**
- Black bead: ~1,086 coins
- Yellow bead: ~1,646 coins
- Red bead: ~1,394 coins
- White bead: ~2,107 coins
- **Total Cost: ~6,233 coins**

**Why Buy?**
- Saves 30-60 minutes of grinding
- Guaranteed to get all beads immediately
- Can complete quest in under 5 minutes

---

**Option B: Kill Imps (Free but Time-Consuming)**

**Drop Rate:** 5/128 per bead (~3.9% chance)
**Average Kills Needed:** ~100-150 imps for all 4 beads

**Best Imp Locations:**

1. **East of Ardougne Monastery** (Members)
   - Many imps spawn here
   - Relatively safe area

2. **Karamja Volcano** (Top of volcano)
   - Multiple imp spawns
   - **Warning:** Level 14 scorpions also spawn here
   - Bring food if low combat

3. **Southern Entrance to Falador** (F2P)
   - Good for F2P players
   - Near bank for storing beads

4. **Kastori Basement** (Members)
   - Underground area with imp spawns

**Imp Behavior:**
- Every 30-120 seconds, 25% chance imp teleports away in smoke
- Use **Magic or Ranged** to reduce downtime
- Imps are level 2 (very weak)

**Recommended Combat:**
- Any combat level can kill imps
- Use Magic for training while collecting beads
- Use Ranged to attack from distance

---

**Option C: Passive Collection**

- Collect beads over time while doing other activities
- Kill imps whenever you encounter them
- Store beads in bank until you have all 4
- Turn in when complete

---

#### Combat Tips for Imp Hunting:

**Best Spells to Use:**
- Wind Strike (level 1 Magic)
- Fire Strike (level 13 Magic)
- Any combat spell for Magic training

**Best Ranged Weapons:**
- Shortbow with bronze/iron arrows
- Crossbow with bronze bolts

**Inventory Setup:**
- Weapon and runes/ammo
- Some food (if low level)
- Empty inventory space for bead drops

---

**Step 3: Complete the Quest**

1. Collect all 4 beads:
   - ✓ Black bead
   - ✓ Yellow bead
   - ✓ Red bead
   - ✓ White bead

2. Return to Wizard Mizgog (2nd floor of Wizards' Tower)

3. Talk to him with all 4 beads in your inventory
   - **Important:** He won't accept them one at a time!
   - **Ultimate Ironmen:** Plan to complete in one trip!

4. He'll take the beads and give you your reward

5. Quest complete!

---

#### Rewards:
- ✅ 1 Quest Point
- ✅ 875 Magic XP
- ✅ **Amulet of Accuracy** (great early-game amulet!)

**Magic Level After Both Quests:**
- Starting from level 1 (7 XP from Tutorial Island)
- Witch's Potion: 325 XP
- Imp Catcher: 875 XP
- **Total: 1,207 XP = Level 8 Magic!**

---

#### Post-Quest: Additional Amulets of Accuracy

After completing the quest, you can get more Amulets of Accuracy:
1. Collect another set of 4 beads (black, yellow, red, white)
2. Talk to Wizard Mizgog with the beads
3. He'll give you another Amulet of Accuracy
4. Repeat as many times as you want!

**Why Get More?**
- Good early-game amulet (+4 Attack bonus in all styles)
- Free to obtain (just kill imps)
- Can give to alt accounts or friends

---

#### Quick Route Summary:

**Fastest Route (5 minutes with Grand Exchange):**
1. Buy all 4 beads from Grand Exchange (~6,233 coins)
2. Teleport to Wizards' Tower
3. Talk to Wizard Mizgog on 2nd floor
4. Complete quest
5. Receive 875 Magic XP + Amulet of Accuracy

**Free Route (30-90 minutes):**
1. Kill imps at chosen location
2. Collect all 4 beads (may take 100+ kills)
3. Go to Wizards' Tower
4. Talk to Wizard Mizgog
5. Complete quest

**Recommended for Ironman:**
- Kill imps while training combat/Magic
- Collect beads passively over time
- Complete when you have all 4

---

#### Combined Quest Strategy:

**Do Both Quests Together for Maximum Efficiency:**

1. **Witch's Potion** (5-10 minutes)
   - Rewards: 325 Magic XP

2. **Imp Catcher** (5-60 minutes)
   - Rewards: 875 Magic XP + Amulet of Accuracy

**Total Rewards:**
- 2 Quest Points
- 1,200 Magic XP (level 1 → level 8!)
- Amulet of Accuracy
- **Total Time:** 10-70 minutes
- **Total Cost:** ~6,250 coins (if buying beads)

**This saves you hours of early Magic training!**

## Early Levels (1-55)

### Levels 1-10: Questing (FASTEST)
**Time:** 10-15 minutes  
**Cost:** Minimal  
**XP Gained:** 1,200 XP

**Quests to Complete:**
1. **Witch's Potion** - 325 XP
2. **Imp Catcher** - 875 XP

**Total:** 1,200 XP = Instant Level 10!

**Why This Method:**
- Both quests are very short
- No requirements
- Saves hours of grinding
- Can be done at any combat level

---

### Levels 7-21: Lvl-1 Enchant
**XP Rate:** ~32,000 XP/hour  
**Cost:** Small profit or loss depending on prices  
**Casts Needed:** 250 casts (7→21)

**Method:**
- Cast **Lvl-1 Enchant** on sapphire or opal jewelry
- Each cast gives 17.5 XP
- Can be cast every 3 ticks (1.8 seconds)

**Runes Required per Cast:**
- 1 Cosmic rune
- 1 Water rune

**Items to Enchant:**
- Sapphire rings, necklaces, or amulets
- Opal rings, necklaces, or amulets

**Tips:**
- Use a staff of water to save on water runes
- Buy jewelry from Grand Exchange in bulk
- Check prices before buying - sometimes profitable!

---

### Levels 21-55: Low Level Alchemy
**XP Rate:** ~60,000 XP/hour (standing still)  
**Cost:** Small loss (usually 10-50 gp per cast)  
**Casts Needed:** 5,214 casts (21→55)

**Method:**
- Cast **Low Level Alchemy** on cheap items
- Each cast gives 31 XP
- Can be cast every 3 ticks (1.8 seconds)
- **Can be done while doing other activities!**

**Runes Required per Cast:**
- 3 Fire runes
- 1 Nature rune

**Best Items to Alch:**
- Rune arrows
- Cheap armor pieces
- Check OSRS Wiki calculator for current best items

**Tips:**
- Use a staff of fire to save on fire runes
- Do this while questing, running, or training Agility
- Place items in 4th row, far left for easy clicking

---

### Alternative: Levels 43-55: Superheat Item
**XP Rate:** ~97,000 XP/hour  
**Cost:** Varies (usually small loss)  
**Bonus:** Also gives Smithing XP!

**Method:**
- Cast **Superheat Item** on ores to make bars
- Each cast gives 53 Magic XP
- Faster than Low Alchemy but costs more

**Best Ores:**
- Iron ore (cheapest)
- Gold ore (with Goldsmith gauntlets for Smithing XP)

---

### Alternative: Levels 45-55: Camelot Teleport
**XP Rate:** ~80,000 XP/hour  
**Cost:** ~225,000 coins (45→55)  
**Casts Needed:** 1,895 casts

**Method:**
- Repeatedly cast **Camelot Teleport**
- Each cast gives 55.5 XP
- Simple and straightforward

**Runes Required per Cast:**
- 5 Air runes
- 1 Law rune

## Passive Training Methods (55+)

### Level 55+: High Level Alchemy (RECOMMENDED)
**XP Rate:** ~78,000 XP/hour (standing still)  
**XP Rate:** ~20,000-50,000 XP/hour (while doing other activities)  
**Cost:** Often PROFITABLE!

**Method:**
- Cast **High Level Alchemy** on items
- Each cast gives 65 XP
- Can be cast every 5 ticks (3 seconds)
- **This is THE passive training method**

**Runes Required per Cast:**
- 5 Fire runes
- 1 Nature rune

**Best Items to Alch:**
- Check the [OSRS Wiki Alchemy Calculator](https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Calculator:Magic/High_Level_Alchemy)
- Common profitable items:
  - Rune items (platelegs, plateskirts, etc.)
  - Dragon hide bodies
  - Battlestaves
  - Various armor pieces

**Tips:**
- **ALWAYS check prices before buying in bulk!**
- Test with 1 item first
- Use a staff of fire to save on fire runes
- Place items in 4th row, far left slot
- Do this while:
  - Questing
  - Training Agility
  - Doing farm runs
  - Training melee
  - Basically any other activity!

**From 55 to 99:**
- Total casts needed: 197,966
- Total time: ~165 hours of alching
- Can make millions in profit with right items!

---

### Level 82+: Magic Imbue (Lunar Spellbook)
**XP Rate:** ~20,000-24,000 XP/hour  
**Cost:** ~2 gp per XP  
**Requires:** Lunar Diplomacy quest

**Method:**
- Cast **Magic Imbue** every 12.6 seconds
- Gives 86 XP per cast
- Does not interrupt other actions
- Mainly used while training Runecraft

## Active Training Methods (62-99)

### Level 62+: Bursting/Barraging (FASTEST)
**Requires:** Desert Treasure I quest (for Ancient Magicks)

#### Overview
- Burst and Barrage spells hit multiple enemies at once
- Extremely fast XP rates
- Can be expensive
- Best done in multi-combat areas

---

### Method 1: Slayer Tasks (RECOMMENDED)
**XP Rate:** 150,000-250,000 XP/hour  
**Cost:** Usually breaks even or profits!  
**Requires:** 65+ Slayer recommended

**Best Monsters to Burst/Barrage:**
1. **Dust Devils** (65 Slayer)
   - Catacombs of Kourend
   - Very popular and profitable
   
2. **Nechryaels** (80 Slayer)
   - Greater Nechryaels in Catacombs
   - Excellent XP and drops

3. **Smoke Devils** (93 Slayer)
   - Smoke Devil Dungeon
   - Great XP rates

4. **Abyssal Demons** (85 Slayer)
   - Requires alt accounts to lure
   - Very good XP

**Spells to Use:**
- **Level 70:** Ice Burst (cheaper)
- **Level 94:** Ice Barrage (fastest)
- **Level 82+:** Use Saturated Heart to boost to 94

**Why This Method:**
- Trains both Magic and Slayer
- Usually profitable from drops
- Most efficient long-term
- Sustainable

---

### Method 2: Maniacal Monkeys (FASTEST XP)
**XP Rate:** 310,000-400,000 XP/hour  
**Cost:** Expensive (runes only)  
**Requires:** Monkey Madness II started (up to Kruk's Dungeon)

**Method:**
- Kill Maniacal Monkeys in Kruk's Dungeon
- They spawn in huge numbers
- Very low Magic defence
- Long aggression range

**Spells to Use:**
- **Ice Burst:** ~310,000 XP/hour
- **Ice Barrage:** ~400,000 XP/hour

**Equipment:**
- Prioritize Magic damage bonus
- Prayer bonus is important
- Bring Prayer potions

**Tips:**
- Use 2 alt accounts to stack monkeys (optional but helpful)
- Very click-intensive
- Expensive but fastest real-time XP

---

### Method 3: Zombie Pirates (Wilderness)
**XP Rate:** 200,000-300,000 XP/hour  
**Cost:** Moderate (offset by drops)  
**Requires:** Medium Wilderness Diary (recommended)

**Location:** Level 10-11 Wilderness near Chaos Altar

**Equipment:**
- **Accursed Sceptre (a)** - 50% damage boost in Wilderness
- **Salve Amulet (ei)** - 20% boost vs undead
- Don't risk items you can't afford to lose!

**Method:**
- Burst/Barrage Zombie Pirates
- Use Protect from Magic prayer
- Escape south if PKers appear

**Drops:**
- Average 4,258 gp per kill (with Medium Diary)
- Can be profitable

**Risks:**
- Player Killers (PKers)
- Can be teleblocked
- Bring minimal risk gear

---

### Method 4: Bandits (The Big Heist Lodge)
**XP Rate:** 150,000-230,000 XP/hour  
**Cost:** Expensive  
**Requires:** Saradomin or Zamorak item

**Method:**
- Stand 2 tiles west of door inside lodge
- Bandits auto-stack for AoE spells
- Use Blood Burst/Barrage for healing

**Spells:**
- **Blood Burst (68 Magic):** 150,000 XP/hour
- **Blood Barrage (92 Magic):** 230,000 XP/hour

**Cost:**
- Bursting: ~1,200,000 gp/hour
- Barraging: ~2,144,400 gp/hour

**Why This Method:**
- Very AFK (similar to Nightmare Zone)
- Blood spells heal you
- Unlikely to die
- No drops to offset cost

---

### Method 5: Killing Crabs (Low Level Alternative)
**XP Rate:** Varies by level  
**Cost:** Cheap  
**Requires:** Low requirements

**Crab Types:**
1. **Ammonite Crabs** (Fossil Island) - 100 HP
2. **Sand Crabs** (Hosidius) - 60 HP
3. **Rock Crabs** (Rellekka) - 50 HP
4. **Frost Crabs** (Varlamore) - 60 HP, 100% fire weakness

**Method:**
- Use highest combat spell available
- Very AFK
- Low Defence, high HP
- Reset aggression every 10 minutes

**Recommended:**
- 30+ Defence to reduce damage taken
- Elemental staff for unlimited runes
- Smoke battlestaff (30 Magic/Attack) for 10% damage boost

## Recommended Path to 99

### For Most Players:
1. **Levels 1-10:** Complete Witch's Potion + Imp Catcher
2. **Levels 10-55:** Low Level Alchemy (passive while questing)
3. **Levels 55-94:** High Level Alchemy (passive, profitable)
4. **Levels 94-99:** Ice Barrage on Slayer tasks

**Total Time:** ~200-250 hours (mostly passive)  
**Total Cost:** Profit or minimal loss

### For Speed (Active Training):
1. **Levels 1-10:** Quests
2. **Levels 10-62:** Fastest methods (alching, teleports)
3. **Levels 62-99:** Maniacal Monkeys bursting/barraging

**Total Time:** ~60-80 hours  
**Total Cost:** 50-100M+

---

## Tips and Tricks

1. **Always use elemental staves** to save on runes
2. **Check prices before buying** alch items in bulk
3. **Do passive training** while doing other activities
4. **Complete Desert Treasure I** as soon as possible
5. **Train Slayer** for sustainable Magic training
6. **Use Runelite plugins** for alch warnings and timers
7. **Don't rush** - passive training is very efficient
8. **Save money** for better gear instead of rushing 99
