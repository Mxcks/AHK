## How It Works

### The Spell:
1. Open spellbook
2. Click **High Level Alchemy** spell
3. Click on item in inventory
4. Item converts to coins
5. Repeat!

### Cast Speed:
- Can cast every **5 ticks** (3 seconds)
- ~1,200 casts per hour standing still
- ~300-800 casts per hour while doing other activities

### XP Calculation:
- 65 XP per cast
- 1,200 casts/hour = 78,000 XP/hour
- 500 casts/hour (passive) = 32,500 XP/hour

## Step-by-Step Setup

### Step 1: Get Required Items

**Runes Needed:**
- Nature runes (1 per cast)
- Fire runes (5 per cast) OR fire staff

**For 1,000 Casts:**
- 1,000 Nature runes
- 5,000 Fire runes (or use fire staff)

**Where to Buy Runes:**
- Grand Exchange (best prices)
- Magic shops (more expensive)
- Runecraft them yourself (Ironman)

---

### Step 2: Find Profitable Alch Items

**Method A: Use OSRS Wiki Calculator**
1. Check calculator for current best items
2. Note the profit per alch
3. Buy 100 of the item to test

**Method B: Common Safe Items**
- Rune platelegs (usually 100-300 gp profit each)
- Rune plateskirts (usually 100-300 gp profit each)
- Magic longbows (usually break-even or small profit)

**üí° Always test with small quantities first!**

---

### Step 3: Buy Items in Bulk

**Grand Exchange Tips:**
1. Check current GE price
2. Calculate alch value
3. Buy at or below profitable price
4. Start with 100-500 items to test
5. Scale up if profitable

**Alch Value Formula:**
- High alch value is fixed for each item
- Profit = (Alch value) - (GE price) - (Nature rune cost)

## Training Methods

### Method 1: Active Alching (78,000 XP/hour)

**Setup:**
1. Stand in one spot (bank, GE, etc.)
2. Open spellbook
3. Click High Alch
4. Click item
5. Repeat

**Best For:**
- Maximum XP per hour
- When you can focus on the game
- Short training sessions

**Recommended Locations:**
- Grand Exchange (near bank)
- Lumbridge (peaceful)
- Your POH (private)

---

### Method 2: Passive Alching (20,000-50,000 XP/hour)

**Do While:**
- Questing
- Training Agility
- Doing farm runs
- Training melee at crabs
- Running between locations
- Watching videos/streams

**How to Passive Alch:**
1. Do your main activity
2. Every 3 seconds, click alch + item
3. Continue main activity
4. Repeat

**Best For:**
- Long-term training
- Efficient use of time
- Not wanting to focus on Magic

---

### Method 3: Alching During Agility (Recommended!)

**Why This is Amazing:**
- Train two skills at once
- Agility has downtime between obstacles
- Perfect timing for alchs
- Very efficient

**How to Do It:**
1. Start an Agility course
2. Between obstacles, cast alch
3. Continue course
4. Repeat

**Best Agility Courses for Alching:**
- Canifis Rooftop Course (40+ Agility)
- Seers' Village (60+ Agility)
- Ardougne (90+ Agility)

## Tips and Tricks

### üí° Pro Tips:

1. **Always check prices before buying in bulk**
   - Prices change hourly
   - Test with 100 items first

2. **Use a fire staff**
   - Saves millions on fire runes
   - Only need Nature runes

3. **Place items in same inventory slot**
   - Muscle memory
   - Faster clicking

4. **Alch while doing other activities**
   - Most efficient use of time
   - Train multiple skills

5. **Use Runelite alch warning**
   - Prevents costly mistakes
   - Shows profit/loss

6. **Buy Nature runes in bulk**
   - Cheaper than buying small amounts
   - Stock up when prices are low

7. **Consider Ironman methods**
   - Craft items yourself
   - Alch your own products

## Common Mistakes

### ‚ùå Mistakes to Avoid:

1. **Buying items without checking calculator**
   - Can lose millions
   - Always verify profitability

2. **Not using a fire staff**
   - Wastes money on fire runes
   - Significantly more expensive

3. **Alching valuable items by accident**
   - Use alch warning plugin
   - Keep valuable items in bank

4. **Buying too many of one item**
   - Prices can crash
   - Diversify your alch items

5. **Not alching passively**
   - Missing out on free XP
   - Alch while doing other things!
