## Step-by-Step (20 Minutes)

### Preparation (5 minutes):
1. Get 7,000 coins total
   - **Need coins?** Do [Stronghold of Security](../../Money%20Making/Early%20Game/First-10000-Coins-Guide.md)
2. Go to Grand Exchange
3. Buy these items:
   - [ ] Eye of newt (3 gp)
   - [ ] Black bead (~1,086 gp)
   - [ ] Yellow bead (~1,646 gp)
   - [ ] Red bead (~1,394 gp)
   - [ ] White bead (~2,107 gp)
4. Bank everything except Eye of newt

---

### Witch's Potion (10 minutes):

**Step 1:** Go to Rimmington (south of Falador)

**Step 2:** Pick onion north of Rimmington

**Step 3:** Go to Hetty's house (south-east Rimmington)
- Talk to Hetty
- Start quest

**Step 4:** Kill level 1 rat in archery shop (west of Hetty)
- Get rat's tail

**Step 5:** Kill cow south of Falador
- Get raw beef
- Burn it on Hetty's fireplace

**Step 6:** Complete quest
- Give Hetty all 4 items
- Drink from cauldron
- **+325 Magic XP!**

**Detailed Guide:** [Witch's Potion Quest](../../Quests/Magic%20Quests/Witchs-Potion-Quest.md)

---

### Imp Catcher (5 minutes):

**Step 1:** Get the 4 beads from bank

**Step 2:** Go to Wizards' Tower (south of Draynor)

**Step 3:** Climb to 2nd floor

**Step 4:** Talk to Wizard Mizgog
- Give him all 4 beads
- **+875 Magic XP!**
- **Get Amulet of Accuracy!**

**Detailed Guide:** [Imp Catcher Quest](../../Quests/Magic%20Quests/Imp-Catcher-Quest.md)

## Tips for Success

### üí° Pro Tips:

**1. Do Stronghold first**
- Gets you all the money you need
- Only takes 10 minutes
- Brings lots of food!

**2. Buy beads, don't kill imps**

### ‚ùå Avoid These:

**1. Trying to kill imps for beads**
