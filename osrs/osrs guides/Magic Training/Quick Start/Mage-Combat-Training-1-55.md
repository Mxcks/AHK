## Combat Spell Progression

### Level 1-13: Wind Strike

**Spell:** Wind Strike  
**Runes:** 1 air, 1 mind (air free with staff!)  
**Damage:** 0-2  
**XP per cast:** 5.5  
**Cost per cast:** 5 gp (mind rune)

**Where to Train:**
- Cows (Lumbridge) - Level 2
- Chickens (Lumbridge) - Level 1
- Goblins (Lumbridge) - Level 2

**XP Rate:** ~8,000-10,000 XP/hour  
**Time to Level 13:** ~2 hours from level 8

**Profit:**
- Cowhides: 100-150 gp each
- Bones: 50-100 gp each
- **Usually profitable!**

---

### Level 13-29: Fire Strike

**Spell:** Fire Strike  
**Runes:** 2 air, 1 mind, 3 fire (air free with staff!)  
**Damage:** 0-8  
**XP per cast:** 11.5  
**Cost per cast:** ~20 gp

**Where to Train:**
- Cows (Lumbridge) - Still good!
- Hill Giants (Edgeville Dungeon) - Level 28
- Moss Giants (Varrock Sewers) - Level 42

**XP Rate:** ~15,000-20,000 XP/hour  
**Time to Level 29:** ~8 hours from level 13

**Tips:**
- Fire Strike is much stronger than Wind Strike
- Kills monsters faster = more XP
- Hill Giants drop big bones (good money!)

---

### Level 29-41: Water Strike

**Spell:** Water Strike  
**Runes:** 2 air, 1 mind, 3 water (air free with staff!)  
**Damage:** 0-10  
**XP per cast:** 12.5  
**Cost per cast:** ~25 gp

**Where to Train:**
- Hill Giants (Edgeville Dungeon)
- Moss Giants (Varrock Sewers)
- Lesser Demons (Wizards' Tower basement)

**XP Rate:** ~18,000-22,000 XP/hour  
**Time to Level 41:** ~6 hours from level 29

**Alternative:** Use Fire Strike if water runes are expensive

---

### Level 41-55: Fire Bolt

**Spell:** Fire Bolt  
**Runes:** 3 air, 4 fire, 1 chaos (air free with staff!)  
**Damage:** 0-12  
**XP per cast:** 22.5  
**Cost per cast:** ~50 gp

**Where to Train:**
- Hill Giants (Edgeville Dungeon)
- Moss Giants (Varrock Sewers)
- Lesser Demons (Wizards' Tower)
- Fire Giants (Waterfall Dungeon)

**XP Rate:** ~25,000-30,000 XP/hour  
**Time to Level 55:** ~10 hours from level 41

**This is your main spell to 55!**

## Money-Making While Training

### Best Drops to Pick Up:

**From Cows:**
- Cowhides (100-150 gp each) - Always pick up!
- Bones (50-100 gp each) - Pick up if space

**From Hill Giants:**
- Big bones (200-300 gp each) - Always!
- Limpwurt roots (500-1,000 gp each) - Always!
- Steel/Iron items - Sometimes

**From Moss Giants:**
- Mossy keys (50,000 gp each) - Rare but valuable!
- Herbs (varies) - Pick up good ones
- Rune items - Very rare but valuable

**Strategy:**
1. Bring small inventory (more space for drops)
2. Pick up valuable drops only
3. Bank when inventory full
4. Sell at Grand Exchange

**You can profit while training!**

## Common Mistakes

### ‚ùå Mistake 1: Not Using Staff
**Problem:** Wasting money on air runes  
**Solution:** Buy air staff for 1,500 gp!

### ‚ùå Mistake 2: Training on High-Level Monsters
**Problem:** Spells splash (miss) too often  
**Solution:** Train on monsters near your combat level

### ‚ùå Mistake 3: Not Picking Up Drops
**Problem:** Losing money  
**Solution:** Always pick up cowhides, big bones, etc.

### ‚ùå Mistake 4: Skipping Quests
**Problem:** Grinding from level 1  
**Solution:** Do Witch's Potion + Imp Catcher first!

### ‚ùå Mistake 5: Using Wrong Spells
**Problem:** Wasting runes on weak spells  
**Solution:** Use highest spell you can (Fire Strike/Bolt)

## Alternative: Enchanting (Faster but Expensive)

### If You Have Money:

**Spell:** Lvl-1 Enchant (Level 7)  
**XP:** 17.5 per cast  
**Cost:** ~50-100 gp per cast  
**XP Rate:** ~20,000-30,000 XP/hour

**Method:**
1. Buy sapphire rings/necklaces
2. Cast Lvl-1 Enchant on them
3. Sell enchanted jewelry
4. Repeat

**Pros:** Faster, AFK-able  
**Cons:** Expensive, boring

**Only recommended if you have 500,000+ gp!**
