## Step 1: Get Your First 10,000 Coins (10 Minutes)

### Where You Are:
- Just finished Tutorial Island
- Standing in Lumbridge
- Have basic tools and a few coins

### What to Do:
**Go to Stronghold of Security!**

**Quick Route:**
1. From Lumbridge, run north toward Varrock
2. When you reach Varrock, run WEST
3. Reach Barbarian Village (west of Varrock)
4. Find hole in ground (center of village)
5. Enter Stronghold

**What You'll Get:**
- 2,500 coins (Floor 1)
- 3,750 coins (Floor 2)
- 5,000 coins (Floor 3)
- Fancy/Fighting boots (Floor 4)
- **Total: 11,250 coins!**

**Tips:**
- Bring 20-30 food (cook some shrimp first)
- Run past all monsters
- Don't fight anything
- Answer security questions (pick safest answers)

**Detailed Guide:** [First 10,000 Coins](../Money%20Making/Early%20Game/First-10000-Coins-Guide.md)

## Step 3: Train to 10 Defence (30-60 Minutes)

### Why 10 Defence?
- Unlocks Black Armor
- Much better protection
- Makes combat easier

### Where to Train:
**Cows in Lumbridge** (Recommended for beginners)

**Location:**
- North-east of Lumbridge
- Cross the bridge
- Enter cow pen

**What to Do:**
1. Attack cows (level 2)
2. Pick up cowhides (sell later!)
3. Train Attack/Strength/Defence
4. Continue until 10 Defence

**Training Mode:**
- Right-click Combat Options
- Set to "Defence" to train Defence
- Or balance between Attack/Strength/Defence

**Time:** 30-60 minutes to reach 10 Defence

**Bonus:** You'll collect ~50-100 cowhides worth 5,000-15,000 gp!

### Now That You Have 10 Defence:

**Shopping Route:**

**Stop 1: Al Kharid** (Start from Lumbridge)

**Stop 2: Varrock** (Walk or teleport north)

**Stop 3: Champions' Guild** (South of Varrock)

- **If you don't have 32 QP:** Skip for now, get later

**Stop 4: Ice Giants** (Optional)
- Kill for Black Kiteshield
- Takes 30-60 minutes
- **Alternative:** Buy from GE or skip

**Total Cost:** ~7,132 gp (without shield)

**Detailed Guide:** [Black Armor Shopping Route](../Equipment/Armor%20Sets/Black-Armor-Shopping-Route.md)

## Important Tips for New Players

### üí° Pro Tips:

**1. Always bank valuable items**
- Don't carry everything around
- Keep coins safe
- Bank armor when not using

**2. Do quests early**
- Huge XP rewards
- Unlock important features
- Much faster than grinding

**3. Don't waste money**
- Save for important purchases
- Don't buy random items
- Focus on gear upgrades

**4. Use the Grand Exchange**
- Buy items you need
- Sell items you don't need
- Check prices before buying

**5. Join a clan**
- Ask questions
- Get help
- Make friends

**6. Use Runelite**
- Free client with helpful features
- Quest helpers
- Skill calculators
- Much better than vanilla client

---

## Common Beginner Mistakes

### ‚ùå Avoid These:

**1. Dying with valuable items**
- Only risk what you can afford to lose
- Bank often
- Don't carry all your money

**2. Training one skill too much**
- Balance your training
- Do quests for variety
- Don't grind one skill to 99 first

**3. Ignoring quests**
- Quests give huge XP rewards
- Much faster than training
- Unlock important content

**4. Buying expensive items too early**
- Focus on affordable upgrades
- Save money for important purchases
- Don't buy cosmetics yet

**5. Not using protection prayers**
- Unlock prayers early
- Use them in combat
- Saves food and time
