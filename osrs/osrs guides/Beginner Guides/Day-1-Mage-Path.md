## Step 1: Get Your First Coins (10 Minutes)

### Goal: Get 6,250+ coins

**Method:** Stronghold of Security (Floors 1-2)

**Quick Instructions:**
1. Run north from Lumbridge toward Varrock
2. When you reach Varrock, run WEST to Barbarian Village
3. Enter the Stronghold hole
4. RUN past all monsters (don't fight!)
5. Collect 2,500 coins (Floor 1)
6. Collect 3,750 coins (Floor 2)
7. **Total: 6,250 coins!**

**Detailed Guide:** [First 10,000 Coins Guide](../Money%20Making/Early%20Game/First-10000-Coins-Guide.md)

## Step 2: Get FREE Wizard Robes (5 Minutes)

**What You'll Get:**
- Wizard hat (FREE!)
- Wizard robe top (FREE!)

- **Total cost: 0 coins!**

**How to Get There:**
1. From Lumbridge, run west to Draynor Village
2. From Draynor, run south to Wizards' Tower

**Alternative - Buy Robes:**

## Step 3: Do Magic Quests (20 Minutes)

### Goal: Get to Level 8 Magic instantly!

**Quests to Complete:**
1. **Witch's Potion** ‚Üí 325 Magic XP
2. **Imp Catcher** ‚Üí 875 Magic XP
3. **Total: 1,200 XP = Level 8 Magic!**

### Quest 1: Witch's Potion (10 minutes)

**Items Needed:**
- Burnt meat (burn raw meat on fire)
- Eye of newt (3 gp at Port Sarim)
- Rat's tail (kill a rat)
- Onion (pick up in field)

**Quick Route:**
1. Talk to Hetty in Rimmington
2. Gather all 4 items
3. Return to Hetty
4. **Reward: 325 Magic XP!**

**Detailed Guide:** [Witch's Potion Quest](../Quests/Magic%20Quests/Witchs-Potion-Quest.md)

### Quest 2: Imp Catcher (10 minutes)

**Items Needed:**
- 4 colored beads (red, yellow, black, white)

**Fast Method (Recommended):**
1. Buy beads from Grand Exchange (~6,000 gp)
2. Talk to Wizard Mizgog (Wizards' Tower, 2nd floor)
3. Give him the beads
4. **Reward: 875 Magic XP + Amulet of Accuracy!**

**Free Method:**
1. Kill imps until you get all 4 beads (30-90 min)
2. Give to Wizard Mizgog

**Detailed Guide:** [Imp Catcher Quest](../Quests/Magic%20Quests/Imp-Catcher-Quest.md)

### ‚úÖ Checkpoint:
- [ ] Witch's Potion complete
- [ ] Imp Catcher complete
- [ ] You are now Level 8 Magic!
- [ ] You have Amulet of Accuracy equipped

## Step 5: Train Magic (30-60 Minutes)

### Goal: Get to Level 13+ Magic

**Training Methods:**

### Method A: Combat Spells (Recommended for Beginners)

**Best Spell:** Wind Strike (Level 1)
- Uses: 1 mind rune, 1 air rune (free with staff!)
- Damage: Up to 2
- XP: 5.5 per cast

**Where to Train:**
1. Cows (Lumbridge) - Level 2, easy
2. Chickens (Lumbridge) - Level 1, very easy
3. Goblins (Lumbridge) - Level 2, easy

**How to Train:**
1. Equip air staff
2. Have mind runes in inventory
3. Right-click monster ‚Üí Cast Wind Strike
4. Kill monsters for XP
5. Pick up drops for money

**XP Rates:**
- ~10,000 XP/hour
- Level 8 ‚Üí 13 in about 30 minutes

### Method B: High Alchemy (Level 55+)

**Not available yet!** Come back when you're level 55.

### Method C: Enchanting (Level 7+)

**Spell:** Lvl-1 Enchant
- Enchant sapphire jewelry
- Can profit or break even
- ~20,000 XP/hour

**More expensive, but faster!**

**Detailed Guide:** [Mage Combat Training 1-55](../Magic%20Training/Quick%20Start/Mage-Combat-Training-1-55.md)

## Common Beginner Mage Mistakes

### ‚ùå Mistake 1: Not Using Air Staff
**Problem:** Wasting money on air runes  
**Solution:** Always use air staff for unlimited air runes!

### ‚ùå Mistake 2: Training on High-Level Monsters
**Problem:** Spells splash (miss) too often  
**Solution:** Train on low-level monsters (cows, chickens)

### ‚ùå Mistake 3: Not Doing Quests First
**Problem:** Grinding from level 1  
**Solution:** Do Witch's Potion + Imp Catcher for instant level 8!

### ‚ùå Mistake 4: Buying Expensive Robes
**Problem:** Wasting money on robes  
**Solution:** Get FREE wizard robes from Wizards' Tower!

### ‚ùå Mistake 5: Running Out of Runes
**Problem:** Can't train without runes  
**Solution:** Buy 1,000+ runes at a time, use air staff
