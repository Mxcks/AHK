## Detailed Comparison

### 1. Starting Costs

**Mage:**
- Wizard robes: FREE (from Wizards' Tower)
- Air staff: 1,500 gp
- Runes: 1,500 gp
- **Total: ~3,000 gp**

**Melee:**
- Black Armor set: 7,000 gp
- Weapon: FREE (bronze from Tutorial Island)
- **Total: ~7,000 gp**

**Winner: Mage (much cheaper!)**

---

### 2. Training Costs

**Mage:**
- Runes for level 1-55: ~50,000-100,000 gp
- Can profit with alchemy at 55+
- Monster drops help offset costs

**Melee:**
- No supply costs
- Monster drops = profit
- Usually profitable from start

**Winner: Melee (free training, profitable)**

---

### 3. Versatility

**Mage:**
- Combat (attack monsters)
- Teleports (travel fast)
- Alchemy (make money)
- Enchanting (make jewelry)
- Required for many quests
- Required for many bosses

**Melee:**
- Combat (attack monsters)
- That's it

**Winner: Mage (way more versatile!)**

---

### 4. Early Game Power

**Mage:**
- Level 1-13: Very weak (2 damage max)
- Level 13-29: Weak (8 damage max)
- Level 29-41: Okay (10 damage max)
- Level 41+: Good (12+ damage)

**Melee:**
- Level 1+: Good damage immediately
- Scales well with better weapons
- Strong from the start

**Winner: Melee (stronger early)**

---

### 5. Late Game Importance

**Mage:**
- ESSENTIAL for teleports
- ESSENTIAL for many bosses
- ESSENTIAL for money making (alchemy)
- ESSENTIAL for quests
- You WILL train this eventually

**Melee:**
- ESSENTIAL for many bosses
- ESSENTIAL for Slayer
- ESSENTIAL for quests
- You WILL train this eventually

**Winner: Tie (both essential!)**

---

### 6. Travel Speed

**Mage:**
- Level 25: Varrock Teleport
- Level 31: Lumbridge Teleport
- Level 37: Falador Teleport
- Level 45: Camelot Teleport
- **Travel anywhere in seconds!**

**Melee:**
- Walk everywhere
- No teleports
- Very slow travel early

**Winner: Mage (teleports are HUGE!)**

---

### 7. Money Making

**Mage:**
- Level 55: High Alchemy (make money while training!)
- Can profit 100,000+ gp/hour
- Passive income while doing other things

**Melee:**
- Monster drops
- Slayer tasks
- Bossing (high level)

**Winner: Mage (alchemy is amazing!)**

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I switch later?
**A:** Yes! You can train both skills anytime. Nothing is permanent.

### Q: Which is better for PvP?
**A:** Both are good! But that's advanced. Start with PvM (fighting monsters).

### Q: Which is better for making money?
**A:** Mage (High Alchemy at 55). Melee is profitable but slower.

### Q: Which is more fun?
**A:** Personal preference! Mage has more variety, melee is simpler.

### Q: Can I be a pure mage forever?
**A:** Yes, but you'll want some melee eventually for certain content.

### Q: Can I be a pure melee forever?
**A:** No, you NEED magic for teleports and many quests.

### Q: What about Ranged?
**A:** That's a third option! But mage or melee are better for beginners.
