### Preparation (5 Minutes) - Get Some Food

**You don't NEED food, but it helps if you get hit!**

**Free Food Method (Recommended):**
1. Go to Lumbridge Swamp (south of Lumbridge)
2. Find a fishing spot (look for bubbles in water)
3. Right-click fishing spot ‚Üí "Net" (catch shrimp)
4. Catch 10-20 shrimp (takes 2 minutes)
5. Go back to Lumbridge
6. Use shrimp on a range/fire (in Lumbridge castle kitchen)
7. Cook all shrimp
8. **You now have 10-20 food!**

**Alternative - Buy Food:**
- Go to Lumbridge General Store
- Buy 10 bread (12 gp each = 120 gp total)
- You start with 25 coins, so you can only buy 2 bread
- **Not recommended - you don't have enough money!**

**Alternative - Skip Food Entirely:**
- Just run fast and don't get hit much
- You might die on Floor 3 or 4
- That's okay! You respawn in Lumbridge
- You keep the coins you already collected!
- Just go back and finish

### Step-by-Step Walkthrough:

**Step 0: Understanding the Strategy**

**KEY POINT: You are NOT going to fight!**
- See a monster? Run past it!
- Getting attacked? Keep running!
- Low health? Eat food while running!
- Don't stop to fight ANYTHING!

**How to Run:**
- Click the boot icon (bottom right) to toggle run
- Or press Ctrl key
- Your energy drains while running
- Walk when energy is low, run when it's high

---

**Step 1: Get to Barbarian Village**

From Lumbridge (where you start):
1. Make sure run is ON (click boot icon)
2. Run north through Lumbridge toward Varrock
3. When you reach Varrock, run WEST
4. You'll reach Barbarian Village (west of Varrock)
5. **Time:** 5-7 minutes running
6. **Your run energy will drain - that's normal!**

**You don't need teleports - just run there!**

**Note:** Barbarian Village is WEST of Varrock, not north!

---

**Step 2: Enter the Stronghold**

1. In Barbarian Village, look for a **hole in the ground**
2. It's in the center of the village
3. Click to climb down
4. You're now in the Stronghold of Security!

---

**Step 3: Floor 1 - War (2,500 coins)**

**Monsters:** Level 5-13 (Minotaurs, Flesh Crawlers)

**What to Do:**
1. **RUN! Don't fight!**
2. Follow the path through the dungeon
3. If a monster attacks you, KEEP RUNNING
4. Answer security questions when you see them
   - Questions are about account security
   - Choose the safest/smartest answers
   - Example: "Never share your password" = correct
5. Find the **Gift of Peace** (ladder down)
6. Click the ladder to go to Floor 2
7. **Reward:** 2,500 coins automatically added to inventory!

**Important:**
- Monsters WILL attack you - that's normal!
- You WILL take damage - that's okay!
- Just keep running to the ladder
- Eat food if health gets low (below 10)
- **You don't need to kill ANYTHING!**

---

**Step 4: Floor 2 - Famine (3,750 coins)**

**Monsters:** Level 25-28 (Catablepon, Zombies)

**What to Do:**
1. **KEEP RUNNING! Still don't fight!**
2. Navigate through the maze
3. Monsters hit harder here - eat food when needed
4. Answer more security questions
5. Find the **Grain of Plenty** (ladder down)
6. **Reward:** 3,750 coins!

**Tips:**
- These monsters hurt more than Floor 1
- Eat food when health drops below 15
- You might need to eat 5-10 food on this floor
- Still don't stop to fight!
- Just run to the ladder

---

**Step 5: Floor 3 - Pestilence (5,000 coins)**

**Monsters:** Level 45-56 (Giant Spiders, Scorpions)

**‚ö†Ô∏è This Floor is DANGEROUS for Level 3!**

**What to Do:**
1. **RUN VERY FAST!**
2. These monsters hit HARD (up to 5-8 damage per hit)
3. Eat food constantly (keep health above 20)
4. Answer security questions
5. Find the **Box of Health** (ladder down)
6. **Reward:** 5,000 coins!

**Important:**
- You WILL get hit a lot here
- You might die - that's okay!
- If you die, you respawn in Lumbridge
- **You keep the 6,250 coins from Floors 1 and 2!**
- You can come back later for Floor 3 and 4
- Or just accept you have 6,250 coins and skip these floors

---

**Step 6: Floor 4 - Death (Fancy/Fighting Boots)**

**Monsters:** Level 68-86 (Ankous, Skeletons)

**‚ö†Ô∏è‚ö†Ô∏è EXTREMELY DANGEROUS FOR LEVEL 3! ‚ö†Ô∏è‚ö†Ô∏è**

**If You Want to Try:**

## Method 2: Kill Cows (SAFEST - 30-60 Minutes)

### If Stronghold Sounds Too Scary:

**This method is 100% safe and easy!**

**Location:** Cow pen in Lumbridge (north-east of starting area)

**What to Do:**
1. Kill cows (level 2 - very weak!)
2. Pick up cowhides
3. Bank cowhides in Lumbridge
4. Sell to Grand Exchange

**Why This is Safe:**
- Cows are level 2 (you can kill them easily!)
- You won't die
- Right next to bank
- No risk at all
- Good combat XP too!

**Profit:**
- Cowhides: ~100-150 gp each
- Need ~70-100 cowhides for 10,000 gp
- **Time:** 30-60 minutes
- **Risk:** None!

**Recommended if:**
- Stronghold sounds too scary
- You want to train combat anyway
- You prefer safe methods
- You have more time

---

### Step-by-Step Cow Killing:

**Step 1: Get to Cows**
1. From Lumbridge, run north-east
2. Cross the bridge
3. Enter the cow pen
4. **Time:** 1 minute

**Step 2: Kill and Collect**
1. Attack a cow (level 2)
2. Wait for it to die
3. Pick up the cowhide
4. Repeat!

**Step 3: Bank Your Hides**
1. When inventory is full (28 hides)
2. Run back to Lumbridge
3. Use the bank (upstairs in castle)
4. Deposit all cowhides
5. Return to cows

**Step 4: Sell Your Hides**

**Option A: Grand Exchange** (Recommended)
1. Go to Grand Exchange (north-west of Varrock)
2. Sell cowhides for ~100-150 gp each
3. Instant money!

**Option B: Tan and Sell**
1. Take hides to Al Kharid tanner
2. Pay 1 gp per hide to tan
3. Sell leather for more profit
4. More work, slightly more profit
