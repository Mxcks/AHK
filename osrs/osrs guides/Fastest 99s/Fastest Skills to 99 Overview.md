# Fastest Skills to 99 - OSRS

## Overview
This guide ranks all OSRS skills by how fast they can be trained to level 99, with estimated time ranges and brief method descriptions.

---

## Top 5 Fastest Skills

### 1. üî• Firemaking
**Time to 99:** 50-70 hours  
**Cost:** Low to Moderate  
**Difficulty:** Easy

**Fastest Method:**
- **Wintertodt** (50+ Firemaking)
  - 250,000-350,000 XP/hour
  - Profitable (supplies and loot)
  - Very popular minigame
  - Solo or group play

**Alternative Methods:**
- Burning logs (any level)
  - Redwood logs: ~450,000 XP/hour
  - Magic logs: ~400,000 XP/hour
  - Expensive but very fast

**Why It's Fast:**
- High XP rates from early levels
- Wintertodt is engaging and profitable
- Logs burn quickly

---

### 2. üç≥ Cooking
**Time to 99:** 40-60 hours  
**Cost:** Low (can be profitable)  
**Difficulty:** Very Easy

**Fastest Method:**
- **Cooking Karambwans** (30+ Cooking)
  - 900,000+ XP/hour with 1-tick method
  - 450,000 XP/hour normal method
  - Profitable or break-even

**Alternative Methods:**
- **Wine of Zamorak** (35+ Cooking)
  - ~480,000 XP/hour
  - Very profitable
  - No burning

- **Cooking on Range** (any level)
  - Sharks, Anglerfish, etc.
  - 300,000-400,000 XP/hour
  - Use Cooking gauntlets to reduce burning

**Why It's Fast:**
- Extremely high XP rates
- Can be very profitable
- Simple and AFK

**Difficulty:** Easy to Moderate

**Fastest Method:**

## Recommended First 99s for New Players

### Best First 99s:
1. **Cooking** - Fast, profitable, easy
2. **Firemaking** - Fast, fun (Wintertodt), profitable
3. **Fletching** - Fast, can be profitable
4. **Thieving** - Fast, free, profitable

### Skills to Avoid as First 99:
- **Prayer** - Too expensive for new players
- **Construction** - Too expensive
- **Slayer** - Takes too long
- **Runecraft** - Takes too long

## Tips for Fast Leveling

1. **Do quests first** - Many give huge XP rewards
2. **Use the best methods** - Don't use outdated guides
3. **Check prices** - Some "expensive" methods can be profitable
4. **Be efficient** - Use Runelite plugins for timers
5. **Don't rush** - Enjoy the game!
6. **Train multiple skills** - Passive training is very efficient
7. **Join a clan** - Get advice and motivation
8. **Set realistic goals** - Don't burn out
